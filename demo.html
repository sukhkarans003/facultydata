About Department

Shaping Future Leaders

The Department of Management Studies is dedicated to delivering high-quality education in business and management. With a strong focus on practical learning, industry exposure, and innovation, we ensure that our students are career-ready.


We combine academic knowledge with real-world applications through projects, internships, and industry collaborations.

Programs Offered

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

A professional program designed to build leadership, strategic thinking, and managerial skills.


Specializations:

  • Marketing Management
  • Financial Management
  • Human Resource Management

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

A foundation program focusing on business fundamentals and professional development.


Key Learning Areas:

  • Business Communication
  • Accounting
  • Marketing
